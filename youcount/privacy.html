Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared

when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

If you are a 'default' user - using the app just to find protests near or at your location - the only information we collect about you is your current location.

This is only gathered while you are inside the app (not in background).

If you also lead protests, and want to register a new protest with our system, we will ask you to register as such, providing your name and email address, to allow us to manage the list of protest creators.

How We Use Your Information

We use the location information we collect from you just to find if there are any protests registered at/near your location.

If you are a protest creator - we use your name & email to allow you to log into the system to create protest records.

We will NOT use your information to send you promotional information about our products and services, nor will be shared it with any 3rd party.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on the App prior to the change becoming effective. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our use of your

information, please contact us at contact@shsh.com
